ABOUT DRY SKIN!

Dry Skin does not have the tendency to lock in natural mositure and oils so when you have dry skin, your pores are literally almost invisible. It may seem like you have a rough complexion and red patches as well less elasticity on your skin. Sometimes your skin can easily crack, peel, flake, or become itchy, but this isn’t always the case. The major key needed to maintain dry skin is moisture, moisture, and lots of moisture.
