Keys To Hair Epitome

Follow these rules for bomb hair 🔑

Ⅰ You are what you eat! Proteins & Biotin are just as good for your hair as it is for you.

Ⅱ Avoid silicones, sulfates, and alcohols in your shampoo and conditioner.

Ⅲ Rinse and condition in cold water. It helps seal in moisture and shine.

Ⅳ Before you swim, rinse your hair with water, so you hair absorbs clean water instead of chlorine.

Ⅴ DON'T wash everyday. You will dry out your hair of natural oils.

Ⅵ Avoid Tight Hair Ties. Your edges will get snatched, LITERALLY.

Ⅶ Dont use too many products. Overly moisturized is a thing.

Ⅷ Upgrade your pillowcase. Silky pillowcases are as good as it sounds.
