Different types of hair should be treated differently...
or is that too obvious?

These hair types range from 1-4, find out yours!

1A

  • no curl pattern
  • straight
  • shiny fine hair
  • grows fast

    • Is Your Hair Oily?
    With 1A hair, its common for hair to become greasy after one day. Your hair may shine a bad way or smell & that isnt cute-trust me. Dry shampoo is your best friend!! Spray the dry shampoo onto your roots so the shampoo can soak up excess oil.
    Is Your Hair Dry?
    Is your hair dull & lifeless? splitends? breaking off? Your hair needs moisture!! Your scalp isnt getting enough oil- the good kind. Stop overwashing and give the blowdryer or straightner a break. Its causing the moisture to escape.
    Products for overall healthy hair
    Want volume or dry shampoo? Try out the brand Living Proof
    Want a bomb blow-dryer? Try out the Dyson brand
